About ChurchPost

Built for the
people of the church

Most social media tools are built for brands chasing engagement metrics. ChurchPost is built for church communications teams who want to focus on ministry — not technology.

Schedule posts across every major platform and generate captions with AI trained on ministry language. But what really sets ChurchPost apart: it connects directly to Planning Center and Google Calendar — pulling in your actual services, events, groups, and registrations as intelligent content prompts.

No more staring at a blank screen wondering what to post. ChurchPost sees what's coming up in your church and suggests content ideas based on what's actually happening.

Features

Everything your team
actually needs

Multi-platform publishing

Post to Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, and TikTok simultaneously — with format-aware content for each platform.

Smart scheduling

Plan your content calendar weeks in advance. Set it and forget it — ChurchPost publishes at exactly the right time.

AI caption writing

Generate captions in seconds using AI tuned to ministry language — warm, welcoming, and never preachy.

Smart media cropping

Upload once — ChurchPost crops your images and videos to the perfect format for every platform automatically.

Planning Center & Google Calendar

Connect Planning Center to pull in services, events, groups, and registrations as AI content prompts. Connect Google Calendar to surface what's coming up — so ChurchPost always knows what to post about next.

Team collaboration

Invite your communications team with role-based access. Editors create, admins approve and schedule.

Smart content suggestions

Your church calendar,
turned into content

ChurchPost integrates with the tools your church already uses. Connect Planning Center and it automatically imports your upcoming services, events, group meetings, and registrations. Connect Google Calendar and it pulls in every event on your church calendar.

The AI uses all of this to suggest post ideas based on what's actually happening at your church — not generic content. A sermon series starting Sunday? ChurchPost suggests a post for it. A registration closing soon? It reminds you to post about it.

Planning Center

Imports services, events, groups, and registrations. Uses real service plans and sermon titles as post inspiration.

Google Calendar

Syncs your church calendar automatically. Every upcoming event becomes a potential post — ChurchPost surfaces them at the right time.

AI content suggestions

The AI reads your calendar data and suggests post ideas, captions, and timing — all based on what's actually happening at your church.
