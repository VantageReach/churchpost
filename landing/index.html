Features

Everything your team
actually needs

Multi-platform publishing

Post to Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, and TikTok simultaneously — with format-aware content for each platform.

Smart scheduling

Plan your content calendar weeks in advance. Set it and forget it — ChurchPost publishes at exactly the right time.

AI caption writing

Generate captions in seconds using AI tuned to ministry language — warm, welcoming, and never preachy.
